How Swaply works

  1. Set your Instagram handle once — your code is saved.
  2. Show your QR code; the other person scans it and taps to connect.
  3. Scan a friend's code with the built-in camera to swap both ways.
  4. Open Your swaps to see everyone who connected and follow them back.

Common questions

Do I need to log into Instagram?
No. Swaply never asks for your Instagram password — you only type your handle.

Does the other person need the app?
No. They can scan your code and connect from their phone's browser.

The camera won't open.
Enable camera access in iOS Settings → Swaply → Camera, then reopen the scanner.

How do I remove my data?
Email us at the address above and we'll delete your handle and swap records. See our Privacy Policy.
