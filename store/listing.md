Show your code. They scan it, tap once, and you've traded handles — no spelling it

HOW IT WORKS
• Set your handle once — your code is ready forever
• Show your QR; they scan and tap to connect
• Scan a friend's code right back with the built-in camera
• See everyone who swapped with you, and follow back with one tap

WHY IT'S FAST
• The other person doesn't even need the app
• Your handle is remembered on your phone, so every swap is one tap
• A single tap opens their profile so you can follow instantly

Private by design: no login required, no ads, no tracking. Your handle is only ever
shared with the person you choose to swap with.

## Naming & trademark (important)
Meta's brand policy forbids third-party app names/icons that use "Insta" or "Gram",
or that imitate Instagram's look. Both "InstaSwap" and the Instagram-style gradient
icon are exposure here and a plausible App Store rejection reason.

Options:
- Keep "InstaSwap" and accept rejection risk (not recommended).
- Rename to something trademark-clean, e.g. SwapTags / HandleSwap / Tradez / Linkup,
  and shift the icon to our own color (still a clean swap glyph, non-Instagram palette).
Recommendation: pick a clean name now; it's a 20-minute change here vs. a rejected
submission after paying the $99.
